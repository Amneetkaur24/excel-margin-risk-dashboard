#Executive Pricing & Margin Risk Dashboard (Excel)


## Objective
To enable leadership to evaluate discount strategies while proactively managing margin risk.

##Business Question
Which discount levels maximize revenue without increasing exposure to margin risk?
##What This Dashboard Delivers
- Revenue, profit, margin, and order KPIs
- Margin risk segmentation (Healthy / Medium / High)
- Discount band impact on profitability
- Category-level revenue and risk analysis
- Order-level drilldown for high-risk cases
- Interactive filtering by region, category, and business unit

##Key Insight

- Medium discounts consistently provide the strongest balance between revenue growth and margin stability.
- High discounts increase revenue but significantly elevate margin risk.

##Tools & Techniques
- Advanced Excel
- PivotTables & PivotCharts
- Slicers & Form Controls
- VBA Macros (record navigation & interactivity)
- Executive dashboard design principles

## Business Value
- Supports pricing and discount decisions by highlighting where revenue growth creates unacceptable margin exposure.
